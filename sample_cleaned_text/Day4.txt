Digital-to-Digital Conversion
•
The conversion of digital data to digital signal
involves three techniques:
•
Line coding
•
Block coding
•
Scrambling.
•
Line coding is always needed; block coding and
scrambling may or may not be needed.
Computer Networks (CS 306), IIT Indore 4.3

--- Page 4 ---
Line Coding
•
Line coding converts a sequence of bits to a digital
signal. At the sender, digital data are encoded into a
digital signal; at the receiver, the digital data are
recreated by decoding the digital signal.

Block Coding
•
Block coding changes a block of m bits into a block
of n bits, where n is larger than m. Block coding is
referred to as an mB/nB encoding technique

Delta Modulation (DM)
•
PCM is a very complex technique. Other techniques
have been developed to reduce the complexity of
PCM.
•
The simplest is delta modulation.
•
PCM finds the value of the signal amplitude for each
sample; DM finds the change from the previous
sample.
